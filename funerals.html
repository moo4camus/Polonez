Funerals

Polonez Restaurant offers a number of options for reasonably priced funeral luncheons. Each of our menu items are prepared fresh and homemade. The luncheons are served buffet style or upon request, can be served family style for an additional cost. Also available are our homemade soups with an additional charge depending on the soup selected.
