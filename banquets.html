Special Event Information

Milwaukee areas best Polish restaurant, Polonez Restaurant, is the perfect venue for your special event as we offers the same traditional homemade Polish and American entrees in addition to food and cheese trays to entertain your guest's taste buds. Weddings, birthday parties, business meetings or special occasions, our banquet facilities are perfect for your event as there are various options on our banquet menu from which you may choose. From food trays to complete dinners, Polonez will make your event special with a Polish flare. With seating up to 100, you can enjoy an intimate social gathering with authentic Polish and American cuisine.
